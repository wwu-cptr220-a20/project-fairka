What is Gyaxo?

Gyaxo is a song playing app that lets users play songs of their favorite artist. There is even a function to select a Genre and it will give you artists from that Genre

Gyaxo Features

  • Search for songs by artist or song name
  • Preview songs
  • Find songs based on artist recommendations

Author: Kaden Fairchild, Michael Stacy, Ivan Guillen
